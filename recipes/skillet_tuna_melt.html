Skillet Tuna Melt

Description

A deconstructed tuna melt = new favorite healthy dinner.

  • 1 large baguette, torn into bite-size pieces
  • 2 cans tuna
  • 1cup shredded cheddar
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp. yellow mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp. fresh chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, combine baguette, tuna, cheddar, mayo, lemon juice, mustard, garlic, and parsley and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Butter an oven-proof skillet and transfer tuna mixture to skillet.
  3. Bake until mixture is warmed through and cheese is melty, 18 to 20 minutes.
